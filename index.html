Fully digitalized production and delivery

We digitalized every single process in production and delivery. Managers just have to work with people – the rest will be done by Dodo IS.

Order tracker

  • Tells what is to be done and when
  • Instantly gets orders from the order platform
  • Sends performance data to store manager interface
  • Notifies about new orders

Motivation boards and audio assistance

  • Highlights key indicators in relation to the benchmarks
  • Informs about success and misperformance
  • Shows the team the realtime feedback

Drivers’ app and motivation board

  • Connects driver to the manager to solve cases
  • Analyzes the order and places it to the right delivery zone and driver
  • Tracks performance and motivates the driver inside
  • Connected to motivation board to show overall results

Data-driven store management

  • Store mananger sees the performance and feedback in the kitchen online
  • Automatical stock calculation. Direct connection to suppliers. Прогнозы по неободимым ингредиентам
  • Store mananger based on Dodo IS workload forecast can manage the shift
